My Personal Story

My passion towards creating this intiative comes from my own experiences.

Rohan Sharma was born on August 29th, 2006, in Etobicoke, Ontario, and his motivation for creating awareness for this initiative comes from the inspiration he received from his fellow classmates at Grade 6 in Tall Pines School. He vividly remembers the true excitement his older colleagues had once they raised enough money through food drives and assemblies to build a school in Kenya. The impact that my peers had on almost 150 children was barrier-breaking. He believes that all individuals have the capability of changing lives and creating positive actions, including YOU. Just imagine your family member living abroad dying of thirst and hunger, you’d voluntarily help as much as you can. Please help our initiative because our initiative is nothing with you! Each dollar and even cent matters because it means so much more to someone else than it does to you. For example, an individual sitting at a restaurant might believe his half-full glass of water is half empty, meanwhile, a thirsty individual in the desert will see that glass as half-full. Please share this initiative with others and always be a leader to change someone’s life.
